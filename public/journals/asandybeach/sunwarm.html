the sun has never felt so bright and warm

The sun shines so bright
in my last moments alive
My soul feels so light
as my problems seem all but nigh

I fear death, like any other
but to be alive is such a bother
So, I apologise to all who I have now parted
to my mother, father and my brother
I've delayed my time for far too long
so now's the time I set out
for a journey I will face alone.
